Get more people contacting your website

== Description ==
The Peppermint Wordpress plugins helps you integrate the https://peppermint.com service by installing the Peppermint chatbot app. It helps you to reach out to your visitors so that you get more people contacting you.

It collects emails and phone numbers from your visitors so that you can get back to them and answer any questions.

It takes less than 1 minute to install on your site and requires no configuration to get it working.

You can try it free for 14 days and see if you get more people contacting you and more sales!

== Installation ==
1) Install the Peppermint plugin by uploading the peppermint directory to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory.
2) Activate the Peppermint plugin through the Plugins menu in WordPress.
3) You will need a Peppermint account to enable plugin. You can register at https://Peppermint.com/app/signup (see the Peppermint privacy and term of service here https://peppermint.com/privacy.html and https://peppermint.com/tos.html).
4) When logged in to Peppermint.com go to settings, and then copy and paste the widget id.
